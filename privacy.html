Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26.06.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trivia Showdown collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app through Discord.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the app through Discord, we may receive limited information from Discord that is necessary for the app to function, including:

  • Your Discord user ID.
  • Your Discord username or display name.
  • Your Discord avatar, if provided by Discord.
  • The Discord server, channel, or activity session where the app is being used.
  • Gameplay data such as player names, scores, buzzes, wagers, answers, game state, and selected question boards.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as payment details, government identification, precise location, health information, or private Discord messages.

2. User-Provided Content

The app may allow users to create, upload, import, or share custom question boards and other game content. This content may include text, images, links, clues, answers, and category names.

You should not upload personal information, confidential information, or content you do not have permission to use.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only as needed to:

  • Run multiplayer trivia games.
  • Identify players during a game session.
  • Display usernames, avatars, scores, buzzes, answers, and game progress.
  • Save or restore active game sessions where supported.
  • Debug errors, improve performance, and maintain app security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and Discord’s developer requirements.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only in the following situations:

  • With Discord, as necessary for the app to operate within Discord.
  • With hosting, database, logging, or infrastructure providers that help us operate the app.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, Discord, or others.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Game session data may be stored temporarily to allow active games to function or be restored after disconnections. Custom boards or imported content may be stored if the app supports saving them.

We aim to keep data only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, provide its features, resolve technical issues, comply with legal obligations, or handle user requests.

6. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of personal information associated with your use of the app by contacting us at fin.yet@gmail.com. Please include your Discord user ID if possible so we can locate the relevant data.

Some information may be retained where required by law, for security reasons, or where deletion is not technically possible from backups or temporary logs immediately.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children and Young Users

The app is intended for use through Discord and is subject to Discord’s age requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children outside what Discord provides for normal app functionality.

9. Third-Party Services

The app runs through Discord and may rely on third-party hosting, storage, analytics, or infrastructure providers. Your use of Discord is also governed by Discord’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

10. International Users

Information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. By using the app, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in locations where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: fin.yet@gmail.com
